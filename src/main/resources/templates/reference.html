📝 Key Constitutional Amendments

1st Amendment (1951)

Added reasonable restrictions on FRs, created 9th Schedule, abolished zamindari

Amendment

42nd Amendment (1976)

"Mini Constitution" — added Fundamental Duties, made India "Socialist, Secular" in Preamble

Amendment

44th Amendment (1978)

Undid excesses of 42nd Amendment — made right to property a legal right (Article 300A)

Amendment

61st Amendment (1989)

Reduced voting age from 21 to 18 years

Amendment

73rd Amendment (1992)

Panchayati Raj — three-tier system, 11th Schedule (29 subjects), reservation for women

Amendment

74th Amendment (1992)

Municipalities — urban local government, 12th Schedule (18 subjects)

Amendment

86th Amendment (2002)

Right to Education — made education a fundamental right (Article 21A) for ages 6-14

Amendment

101st Amendment (2016)

GST — Goods and Services Tax, one nation one tax

Amendment

103rd Amendment (2019)

EWS Reservation — 10% reservation for Economically Weaker Sections in general category

Amendment
